ABOUT ME


After earning my post-doctoral in Physics from University of São Paulo, I entered the Python world to explore my passion for programming and data analysis. Physics corroborated my critical and creative analytical thinking, which makes me an adaptive professional in several areas and groups. I worked for over 12 years in multidisciplinary groups, being considered a noteworthy in the academic research space. During my research fellowship period at Molecular Biology Institute of Paraná (IBMP) I helped to design and execute from end to end the point-of-care diagnostic system, proposed a technology roadmap change for the chip design and improved the entire system. I wrote the diagnostic system patent and the equipment is widely used for biological researching at the institute ever since. Last month I completed a 36 hours UX Design course, increasing my skills in this area which were acquired in my job with the diagnostic system. I am an art lover, and musician, and 1st Dan black belt in Karate by Nihon Karate Kyokai, and I am doing my best to learn Japanese, starting my studies shortly. I speak a native Portuguese and an advanced English.
